Mission Statement

Community Tutoring (CT) is an organization that builds on the blueprint of Boston Community Tutoring (formerly Boston COVID-Tutoring) to expand educational support to communities worldwide. Originating from a 501(c)3 nonprofit organization founded by high school students from the Buckingham Browne & Nichols School in Cambridge, MA, CT aims to provide free online tutoring to elementary, middle, and high school students globally. By addressing the academic gaps caused by disruptions in traditional schooling, CT ensures that all students, regardless of their background, can achieve their educational goals. We believe every child deserves the opportunity to expand their knowledge and pursue their academic passions, recognizing that education is a powerful tool that shapes their future from career choices to lifelong learning.

Our mission is to provide free, quality, and accessible online tutoring to all elementary and middle school students regardless of their economic and immigration status, race, sex, etc. in communities throughout the world.
